RadicalRush! 2.0

Two more modes in RadicalRush in this version! The Survival mode is just to test out your skills on how long you can survive. The Time Attack is to see how many radicals you can destroy by maneuvering them and them running each other in 60 seconds! Also, in this version there is no shooting, and the player moves towards the mouse's position!


Try it out!

Town Invasion

An invasion game where you have to instantiate your minions and have them get to the other side! You can instantiate as many as you want, but these minions have lower health than the AI's minions, and are slower than the enemy's minion. Try to beat the enemy in 20 seconds!

Try it out!

King of the Light

This game is a King of the Hill-themed game. The objective of the game is to grab the light, and keep holding on the light for 30 seconds. You do not want to have the opponent take possession of the light. Whoever has the light after 30 seconds wins!


Try it out!

Invasive Bacterium

Invasive Bacterium is a game where you are playing defense and you do not want the Bacterium to enter your side. Every time you successfully shoot a bacterium, you get one point. However, if they are able to get to the other side, they get a point. Whoever gets the most points after 30 seconds wins!

Try it out!

ForceRun! 2D

This is a simple avoid-the-obstacle game. You are supposed to dodge the blocks that are falling down. However, the player movement is different - everytime you press A or D, there is an invisible force added to the object, so you need to be careful to not go too fast.


Try it out!

RadicalRush!

This game is kind of like a invasion / defend-the-castle game. Your goal is to destroy free radicals in the body. You have to shoot out antidioxidants in order to destroy them. If you die, you lose.


Try it out!

Dropping Boxes / Dropping Crates

This is a simple dodging game. You are the block, trying to avoid the blocks that are falling down from the sky. Every second you survive, adds a point to the score.


Try it out!
